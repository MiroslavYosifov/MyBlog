Space Missions

  1. 11 November 1935 USA

    The Explorer II bolloon takes two people to 22,066 metres (72,395ft) where they observe and photograph the curvature of the Earth

  2. 24 October 1946 USA

    First pictures of Earth from 105km (65 miles).

    V2 rocket

  3. 20 February 1947

    First animals in space (fruit flies)

    V2 rocket

  4. 4 October 1957 USSR

    First artificial satellite. First signal from space.

    Mission-Sputnik 1

  5. 3 November 1957 USSR

    The first living creature was sent into space. Laiky dog was strapped into Sputnik 2.

  6. 31 January 1958 USA

    Comfirmed the existence of the Van Allend belts

    Mission-Explorer 1

  7. 4 January 1959 USSR

    First artificial satellite to reach the Moon vicinity and first artificial satellite in heliocentric orbit

    Mission-Luna 1

  8. 7 August 1959 USA (NASA)

    First photograph of Earth from orbit.

    Mission-Explorer 6

  9. 13 September 1959 USSR

    First impact into another world (the Moon). First delivery of national (USSR) pennants to a celestial body

    Mission-Luna 2

  10. 4 October 1959 USSR

    First photos of another world from space: The far side of the Moon

    Mission-Luna 3

  11. March 1960 USA (NASA)

    First solar probe.

    Mission-Pioneer 5
